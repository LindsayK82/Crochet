About The Whimsical Hooker

Lindsay Klug is a crocheter based out of Ohio. Born and raised in west Texas, she holds an affinity for the desert.

Lindsay has been crocheting since 2011. After discovering the joy of finishing a beanie, she has been unstoppable and now crafts original patterns for others to make.

Other interests include nature walks, embroidery, writing, wood burning, sewing, quilting, and learning to play the banjo.

Lindsay has ADHD, if you haven't figured that out yet. The hyperfixation of crocheting has been an ongoing one, and is now her favorite hobby.

Lindsay welcomes any and all to join her on this amazing crochet journey!
